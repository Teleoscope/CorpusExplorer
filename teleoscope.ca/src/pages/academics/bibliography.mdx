## Bibliography

Teleoscope is supported by a variety of computational systems. At its core, Teleoscope is a:
- [distributed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Distributed_computing) and [cloud-first](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cloud_computing)
- [machine-learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning) system that uses
- [semi-supervised](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weak_supervision) [topic modelling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topic_model)

We use a large variety of frameworks in Teleoscope. Here are the most important:

- [UMAP](https://umap-learn.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)
- [HDBSCAN](https://hdbscan.readthedocs.io/en/latest/how_hdbscan_works.html)
- [RabbitMQ](https://www.rabbitmq.com/)
- [Celery](https://docs.celeryq.dev/en/stable/getting-started/introduction.html)
- [FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/)
- [Milvus](https://milvus.io/)
- [NextJS](https://nextjs.org/)
- [Nextra](https://nextra.site/)
- [Reactflow](https://reactflow.dev/)
